Story mode

This is a feature that allows users to engage with your website by presenting them with a narrative or storyline. It can help make the website more interactive and memorable for users.

Transactional Purpose

This refers to the primary function of your website, which is to facilitate transactions. This could include things like purchases, payments, or other forms of financial exchange.

User Experience (UX)

This is the overall experience that users have when interacting with your website. It encompasses everything from design and layout to functionality and ease of use.

Responsive Design

This means that your website is designed to be easily viewable and accessible across a variety of devices, including desktop computers, tablets, and smartphones.

Security Measures

Since your website will be facilitating transactions, it's important to have strong security measures in place to protect users' personal and financial information.
